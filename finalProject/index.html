Lin Sanity

Jeremy Lin grew up in Palo Alto, California. People had always doubted him when he was a young boy. He was born to two Taiwanese immigrants, and when people looked at his foreign ethnicity, they, unfortunately, doubted that he could ever play basketball. Lin learned the game of basketball from his father, who would teach him at the local YMCA. Even though Lin seemed to have some natural ability, he wasn't tall, which was one reason why people wrote him off. However, Jeremy Lin's performance during 2011-2012 season in New York, changed their minds.
